# Supply Chain Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes supply chain performance using Microsoft Excel.

The dashboard summarizes key metrics including revenue, products sold, and category performance, allowing users to monitor overall business performance through interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Objective

To identify the highest-performing product categories and provide insights that support inventory planning and sales decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Interactive Dashboard
- Category Comparisons

---

## Key Findings

- Skincare generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
- Skincare also recorded the highest number of products sold.
- Haircare ranked second in both revenue and sales volume.
- Cosmetics generated the lowest revenue of the three product categories.
- Overall sales exceeded 46,000 products, generating approximately 577,000 in total revenue.
